# Interview Preparation

Interview preparation for cloud engineering, DevOps, and SRE roles. Each section covers questions you should be able to answer, plus the underlying concepts you need to understand — not just memorize.

---

## How to Use This Section

- **Study the concepts, not the answers** — interviewers probe depth by asking follow-up questions
- **Draw diagrams when explaining architecture** — it shows you think visually and can communicate clearly
- **Give concrete examples from your experience** — "I once..." is more convincing than "one could..."
- **Say what you don't know** — "I haven't worked with X but I'd approach it by..." is better than guessing
