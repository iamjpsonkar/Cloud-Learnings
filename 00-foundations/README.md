## Why This Matters

Cloud certifications and job interviews test these concepts heavily. More importantly, getting these fundamentals wrong leads to costly architectural mistakes:

- Choosing the wrong deployment model locks you into constraints you'll fight for years
- Misunderstanding shared responsibility leads to security gaps
- Ignoring billing models leads to unexpected cost spikes
- Not understanding availability zones leads to single-points-of-failure in production

Work through these before diving into provider-specific services.
